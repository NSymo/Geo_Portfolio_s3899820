Cartography

Cartography consists of creating maps using spatial data obtained from sources such as publicly online government data and population numbers from the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS). Earth Observation Science (AGT)

At RMIT, the Earth Observation Science subject focuses on the use of satellite and aerial remote sensing technologies to collect, analyze, and interpret data about the Earth's surface. This subject typically covers key topics such as:
  • Geospatial Analysis
  • Satellite Imaging
  • Remote sensing

In Earth Observation at RMIT, I analyzed satellite imagery and created classification maps using JavaScript and Google Earth Engine. This involved processing remote sensing data,performing spatial analysis, and classifying land cover or other environmental features based on spectral signatures. I worked with large datasets,applied machine learning algorithms for classification, and gained insights into environmental changes or patterns visible from space.

Industry Experience
This page will briefly cover all my work experience (as of September 2022) within the geospatial science industry. The two main companies that I have worked at were Cardinia Shire and Spiire. This page will outline my experience gained and skills I have learned from working at AAM Group and Tetra Tech along with an overview of my current geospatial skillset.

Cardinia Shire Council

Geospatial Intern
July 2023 - March 2024
During my 240-hour work experience at Cardinia Shire Council, I gained substantial insights into the operations and standards of the geospatial industry. The Council is deeply involved in geospatial activities and specializes in a broad array of geospatial services as a core part of its business. My focus was on asset management, which required me to actively participate in the capture and collection of spatial data. This data was crucial for the mapping team to process and transform into formats usable for various client projects, allowing me to contribute directly to workplace operations.

Spiire

Surveyors Assistant
January 2022 - July 2022
As a surveyor's assistant at SPIIRE, I balanced a mix of field and office responsibilities. In the field, I assisted with various surveying tasks, helping to gather and verify spatial data essential for projects. In the office, my duties included drafting detailed drawings and maps using CivilCAD, ensuring accuracy and compliance with project specifications. Additionally, I actively participated in weekly meetings every Friday, where I engaged in discussions that shaped project directions and workflows.

In my drafting duties at SPIIRE, I focused on creating survey plans for new developments. This involved translating field measurements into detailed and accurate drawings, essential for the planning and execution of construction projects.
